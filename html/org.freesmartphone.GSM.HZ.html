org.freesmartphone.GSM.HZ

Description

The Home Zone interface allows you to be informed about the home zone status. Home Zone is a marketing instrument that is used by several operators. By defining so called 'home zones' on your SIM plus, they adjust how much they charge for your phone calls.

GetHomeZoneStatus ( ) → s

Description: Get current zone name.

Returns

s: zone

The home zone you are currently on. An empty string denotes that you are out of all home zones. "unknown" indicates that the system has not processed the necessary data to compute this information.
